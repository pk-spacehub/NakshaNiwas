FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Naksha Niwas.

What services does Naksha Niwas offer?
We offer plot planning, residential & commercial building design, interior design, 2D & 3D designs, Vastu-based design, turnkey construction, structural analysis, renovation, landscape design, cost estimation, site supervision, approval drawings, smart home planning, and construction consultancy.
How long does it take to get a design ready?
A basic 2D floor plan is typically ready in 3–7 working days. Full architectural drawings with 3D renders take 10–21 days depending on project complexity. Vastu consultations and revisions are included in the timeline.
Do you provide Vastu-compliant designs?
Yes! Vastu-based design is one of our core specialties. Every design we create considers Vastu Shastra principles — from room orientation to entry placement, ensuring positive energy and harmony in your home.
Do you handle municipal approval drawings?
Absolutely. We prepare all municipal approval drawings as per local body requirements and can assist in submitting and following up with authorities on your behalf for a smooth approval process.
Can you design for a small plot like 30×40 ft?
Absolutely! We specialize in compact plot designs. Our team is skilled at maximizing every square foot — creating functional, beautiful homes on plots as small as 20×30 ft without compromising on style or comfort.
What is a turnkey construction contract?
A turnkey contract means we take full responsibility for your project — from design and approvals to construction and interior finishing. You hand over the keys at the start and receive a complete, ready-to-move-in property at the end.
How can I get a cost estimate for my project?
Simply fill out our contact form or WhatsApp us with your plot dimensions, desired number of floors, and requirements. We'll provide a preliminary estimate within 24–48 hours at no charge.
